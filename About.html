About

We are LifeStoree. We want to create a site
where everyone can share their life stories and
communicate with each other. LifeStoree is the new
form of social media. Anyone can create an account
and read stories to find inspiration or
encouragement. You can also share your favorite
stories to your friends on LifeStoree and send
messages. Share your life story. Join LifeStoree.
LifeStoree started from the idea of an 11-year-
old. Since he presented it to a Noble class that had
inspired him to follow his idea, he has worked with
an team of amazing 8th-graders and been advised
by great teachers to create this awesome site to
inspire people everywhere. Welcome to LifeStoree.
